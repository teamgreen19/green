About the Website

This website is to give insight on those interested in different sports categories as well as hobbies and interests. You can also stop by the shop to pick out some merchandise for your favorite teams and sports.




Football

Football is just so fun and it trains you to be faster, stronger and gain more stamina. Football is so well known in the United States and pretty much everyone wants to play. Football can help get scholarships.



Soccer

I love to play soccer because it almost feels therapeutic for me. I like to play because it is also an international sport, so I can play with almost anyone from anywhere.



Volleyball

I love volleyball because it’s a stress reliever. While I only played for 2 years, it was still very fun. It’s a great social activity that teaches teamwork and communication.



Cheer Leading

My name is Marshalia Ward and I've been cheering since I was in the 7th grade. Cheerleading is fun because you get to go to other schools to meet other cheerleaders and watch the games. Also you will become known more around the school. Cheer is amazing and others should try!



Band

My name is Jamea Priest and I've been playing music since elementary school, since I've grown up, band has played a major part in my life and developing who I am today. Band allows many like me to express themselves through an instrument when words fail. Band is not cheap, roughly between $500-millions of dollars, depending on your preference of instrument, band, and quality. Band offers many scholarships as well as money from opportunities such as schools, commercials, performances, and etc. I really do recommend band to anyone looking for a physical activity that strengthens your body as well as mind.



Boxing

I like to box because it is fun and it helps with learning how to fight. You may never know when you have to fight but it also helps me to get stronger.
